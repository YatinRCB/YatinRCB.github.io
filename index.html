Team Sketches: [Bikeshare app ideas]

The first sketch shows an illustrated idea of what the bike share app will look like when you open it up. It will have a login/register page followed by payment info and a student ID number. It will also have a UI showing how many bikes are available at certain bike stations. The second sketch shows more or less of the same things as the first one but also has a page for the user profile and settings. In the settings menu you can access messages, payment information, past trips, billing statements, and password change. Lastly, the final sketch builds off the ideas of the first two but also includes a map and a bus schedule for user convenience.
